About Rassaweck Vineyards

Generously donated by the owner Joe Liesfield, Rassaweck Vineyards is a classicaly beautiful natural venue for our event. Upon arrival, please use the Scotts Brg Rd entrance and drive towards the barn near the dropped pin. Someone will be there to direct you to available parking spaces.

Event Schedule, October 25th 2025

  • 5:00pm - 5:45pm, Guest Arrival
  • 6:00pm, Ceremony
  • 6:30pm, Cocktail Hour, Photography Session
  • 7:30pm - 11:00pm, Reception
